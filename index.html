This is a visualization of the revenues and budgets of 4803 movies.

Here is a quick guide on how to get started.
To change the number of movies you want to see at a time, edit the "Number of Movies" text field.
To change the type of sorting, select one of the three buttons. (Budget, Revenue, and Profit/Budget or Profit Ratio)
If you want to hide a movie, you can click on the bar to remove it from the selection.
As stated in the legend, the red bar represents the budget of the movie while the blue bar represents the profit of the movie.
You can hover over a bar to learn more about a movie via the tooltip.


  • Many of the most profitable movies did not rely on CGI as that would have ate into their budget.
  • Even very successful movies did not score very well in the user ratings. Not many movies score above 8/10.
  • Movies with the highest budget were fantasy ones, a majority of these being superhero movies.
  • Movies with the highest revenue usually had extremely low budgets.
